Treasure Hunt

Are you ready for a treasure hunt? Follow the clues to find the hidden treasure:

  1. Find the palm tree with the skull and crossbones.
  2. Walk 10 paces due north.
  3. Turn left and walk 5 paces.
  4. Look for the big rock with a red X on it.
  5. Dig beneath the rock to find the treasure!
